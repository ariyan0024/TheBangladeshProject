About the Project

The Bangladesh Project is a platform that documents and organizes publicly available online content, focusing on harmful or inappropriate behavior such as abusive comments, harassment, and misleading information. The purpose of the project is to keep a record of such activity in one place, making it easier to observe patterns and understand how this type of behavior appears and spreads online. Submissions are open, but all entries are reviewed before being added. Each entry represents a documented instance involving a specific individual and their public content. The project aims to create a structured archive of online behavior without unnecessary noise, keeping the focus on clarity and documentation.
